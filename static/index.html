🔌 Connect to your Anki App

This website sends AI-generated flashcards directly to the Anki application running on your computer. To allow this connection, please do the following one-time setup:

  1. Open the Anki app on your computer.
  2. Go to Tools at the top menu → Add-ons.
  3. Select AnkiConnect from the list and click the Config button.
  4. Scroll down to webCorsOriginList and add a "*" so it looks exactly like the box below.
  5. Restart your Anki app.
"webCorsOriginList": [
    "http://localhost",
    "*"
]
